About Motion Powered Games

Our Vision

Motion Powered Games aims to revolutionize online gaming by combining video chat with motion tracking technology. We believe that gaming should be an immersive, physical experience that brings people together, even when they're far apart.

How It Works

Video Chat

Our platform uses WebRTC technology to establish peer-to-peer video connections between players. This ensures low-latency communication, essential for responsive gameplay.

Motion Tracking

Using advanced computer vision algorithms, we track your hand movements and facial expressions in real-time. These movements are translated into in-game actions, allowing you to control your character naturally.

Avatar System

Choose from a variety of avatar skins that overlay your video feed. Your avatar will mimic your movements, creating a fun and personalized gaming experience.

Game Mechanics

Engage in boxing matches with friends, where your physical movements determine your success. Throw punches, block attacks, and dodge blows using your actual body movements.

System Requirements

  • Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge recommended)
  • Webcam with at least 720p resolution
  • Stable internet connection (5+ Mbps recommended)
  • Well-lit environment for optimal motion tracking
  • Sufficient space to move your arms freely
